01 — The Opportunity

The world is slowing down. On purpose.

Listening bars — rooted in Japan's post-war jazz kissa tradition — are the fastest-growing hospitality concept globally. Named the #1 cocktail trend for 2025–2026. Luxury brands, cultural institutions, and the world's best bartenders have embraced the format.

Guanajuato Capital — a UNESCO World Heritage City where international tourism has doubled since 2019 — has zero premium cocktail bars, zero listening bars, zero Japanese hospitality concepts. Sin Prisa fills that void.

Japanese listening bar interiors — vinyl walls, warm wood, candlelight

02 — The Experience

Not a bar. An experience.

Owner-curated vinyl through high-fidelity speakers — never algorithmic

The Sound: Purpose-built HiFi. 500+ records — jazz, soul, bossa nova, ambient, city pop. Every selection intentional.

The Space: 20 seats in colonial stone. Warm wood, brass, 2700K lighting. No televisions. A discreet ensō mark at the entrance.

The Service: Omotenashi — Japanese anticipatory hospitality. Quiet, precise, warm.
